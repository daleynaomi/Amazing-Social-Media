Who Is Beyoncé?

Beyoncé Giselle Knowles-Carter is an American singer, songwriter, actress, and record producer.She is known for having multiple hit songs and albums, from her Destiny's Child days to her solo days.She holds the title of the black woman with the most Grammy awards and has been noted as a modern-day icon.
